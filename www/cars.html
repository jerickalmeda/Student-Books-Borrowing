Maintenance Schedule

Every 3,000-5,000 miles

Oil Change

Regular oil changes are crucial for engine health and longevity.

Every 6 months

Tire Rotation

Brake Inspection

Check brake pads, rotors, and fluid levels for safety.

DIY Maintenance Tips

Check Fluids

Regularly check oil, coolant, brake fluid, and windshield washer fluid levels.
